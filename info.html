Who are we?

Who are we ? A group of friends that became a group of runners. The majority of us is in Prague and our Trophy room will attest that... But we also have members in France and in Estonia. We hope and aim to have members all around the world. Our goal is to bring people together and make running (and sport in general) accesible for all. We want to show that everything is possible and we want to share social experiences. Feel free to join the club through the link below.
